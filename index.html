Format Your Document

How to use:

1. In your Google Doc, mark text with symbols like [this] or {this}

2. Copy and paste your document below

3. Tell us what each symbol means in plain English

Example: "[] means make it bold" or "{} means make it italic"



Example Document:

This is [very important information] that everyone should know.
The {key findings} from our research are listed below.
Please for the project.

Example Instructions:

[] means make it bold
{} means make it italic
<> means underline it
